# Apple Cinnamon French Toast Poppables

Freezer-friendly breakfast bites and a smart way to use up leftover bread (including crusts).

## Ingredients

### Custard Base
- 8 eggs  
- 2 cups milk  
- 3/4 cup brown sugar  
- 1 tbsp cinnamon  
- Splash of vanilla  
- Dried bread cubes (enough to soak up the custard)  
- 2 cups peeled and finely chopped apples  

### Crumb Topping
- 3/4 cup brown sugar  
- 3/4 cup flour  
- 1 tsp cinnamon  
- 2–3 tbsp melted butter or margarine (enough to make a “wet sand” texture)  

---

## Instructions

1. Cut leftover bread (or crusts) into cubes and let them dry out overnight.
2. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, milk, brown sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla.
3. Fold in the dried bread cubes and chopped apples. Add just enough bread so the liquid is absorbed.
4. Divide mixture into **24 greased muffin tins** (or use silicone pans).
5. Mix the crumb topping ingredients and sprinkle over the top.
6. Bake at **350°F** for **25 minutes**.
7. Cool completely before freezing. Reheat in the microwave or toaster oven.
