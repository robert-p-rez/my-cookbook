# Sweet Potato Coffee Cake

Moist, warmly spiced coffee cake made with mashed sweet potatoes and topped with a cinnamon crumb.

## Ingredients

### Cake Batter
- 3/4 cup cane sugar  
- 1/2 cup melted butter  
- 2 eggs  
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup  
- 2 cups mashed sweet potatoes (about 2 large; boiled, peeled, and mashed)  
- 2 cups all-purpose flour  
- 2 teaspoons baking powder  
- 2 teaspoons pumpkin spice  
- 1/4 teaspoon salt  

### Cinnamon Layer
- 2 tablespoons coconut sugar or brown sugar  
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon  

### Crumb Topping
- 5 tablespoons flour  
- 1/4 cup coconut sugar or brown sugar  
- 1/4 cup cane sugar  
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon  
- 1/4 cup softened butter  

---

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to **350°F**. Line a 9x9-inch baking dish.

2. In a large bowl, combine cane sugar, melted butter, eggs, maple syrup, and mashed sweet potatoes. Mix with a hand mixer for about 1 minute. (Some small sweet potato clumps are fine.)

3. Add flour, baking powder, pumpkin spice, and salt. Stir until a thick batter forms.

4. Pour half of the batter into the prepared pan.  
   Sprinkle evenly with coconut or brown sugar and cinnamon.  
   Pour the remaining batter over the top.

5. In a small bowl, mix flour, sugars, cinnamon, and softened butter until crumbly. Sprinkle over the batter.

6. Bake for **45 minutes**, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.

7. Cool on a wire rack for about 20 minutes before slicing and serving.
