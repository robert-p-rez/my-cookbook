# Snickerdoodle Bars

## Ingredients

- 3/4 cup unsalted butter, fully melted  
- 3/4 cup packed brown sugar  
- 1 cup granulated sugar  
- 2 large eggs  
- 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract  
- 2 cups all-purpose flour  
- 1 teaspoon baking powder  
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar  
- 1/2 teaspoon salt  

### Topping

- 1/4 cup granulated sugar  
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon  

---

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to **350°F (175°C)**.
2. In a large bowl, combine melted butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar. Mix well.
3. Add eggs and vanilla extract. Mix until fully incorporated.
4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, cream of tartar, and salt.
5. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until combined.
6. Line a **9x9-inch baking dish** with parchment paper.
7. Evenly spread batter into prepared baking dish.
8. In a small bowl, mix together granulated sugar and cinnamon for the topping.
9. Sprinkle topping evenly over the batter.
10. Bake for **27 minutes**, or until set and lightly golden.
11. Allow to cool completely before slicing.

---


## Yield

- Makes **8 large bars**
