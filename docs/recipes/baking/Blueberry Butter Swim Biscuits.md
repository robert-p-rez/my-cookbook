# Blueberry Butter Swim Biscuits

Soft, buttery buttermilk biscuits baked in a butter bath and topped with a sweet glaze.

## Ingredients (makes ~9 biscuits)

### Butter Bath
- 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, melted  

### Biscuit Dough
- 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour  
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar  
- 1 tablespoon baking powder (aluminum-free preferred)  
- 1/2 teaspoon salt  
- 1 3/4 cups buttermilk (add a splash more if dough seems dry)  
- 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen blueberries  

### Glaze (optional but recommended)
- 1 cup powdered sugar  
- 2–3 tablespoons milk  

---

## Instructions

1. **Preheat & prep:**  
   Preheat oven to **450°F (232°C)**. Lightly grease a 9x9-inch (or 8x8-inch) baking dish.

2. **Butter bath:**  
   Pour melted butter into the baking dish, swirling to coat the bottom evenly.

3. **Mix dry ingredients:**  
   In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.

4. **Add buttermilk:**  
   Stir in buttermilk until a sticky dough forms. Add a splash more if needed.

5. **Fold in blueberries:**  
   Gently mix in blueberries to avoid bursting.

6. **Bake:**  
   Spoon the dough over the melted butter in the dish and spread gently.  
   Lightly score into 9 squares with a knife.  
   Bake on the middle rack for **25–30 minutes**, until golden and a toothpick inserted comes out clean.

7. **Glaze & serve:**  
   While baking, whisk powdered sugar and milk to desired drizzling consistency.  
   Drizzle over warm biscuits. Let cool slightly, slice, and serve.
