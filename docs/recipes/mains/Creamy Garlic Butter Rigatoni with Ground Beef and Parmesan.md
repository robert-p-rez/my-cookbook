# Creamy Garlic Butter Rigatoni with Ground Beef and Parmesan

Rich, creamy pasta with seasoned ground beef, garlic butter sauce, and Parmesan.

## Ingredients

- 8 ounces rigatoni (or preferred pasta)

### Beef & Sauce
- 1 lb ground beef  
- 1 teaspoon dried Italian seasoning  
- 1 1/2 cups heavy cream (adjust for desired creaminess)  
- 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese  
- 1 teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)  
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper  
- 2 tablespoons butter  
- 4 cloves garlic, minced  
- 1 medium onion, finely chopped  
- 2 1/2 cups beef broth (adjust as needed for liquid)  

### Garnish
- Fresh chopped parsley (optional)  

---

## Instructions

1. In a large pot over medium heat, melt the butter.

2. Add ground beef and cook, breaking it up with a spoon, until browned and fully cooked.

3. Stir in minced garlic and chopped onion. Cook for 2–3 minutes until softened and fragrant.

4. Sprinkle in Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Adjust seasoning to taste.

5. Pour in beef broth and heavy cream. Stir to combine and bring to a gentle simmer.

6. Add rigatoni, ensuring it is fully submerged in the liquid. Simmer, stirring occasionally, for **15–20 minutes**, until pasta is tender and sauce thickens. Add more broth if needed.

7. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and cook for another 1–2 minutes until sauce becomes creamy.

8. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired. Serve warm.

---

**Total Time:** 30–35 minutes  
**Calories:** ~450 per serving
