# The Coziest Dutch Oven Chicken Dinner

Creamy, cozy Dutch oven chicken with baby potatoes and green beans. Perfect served over egg noodles.

---

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil  
- 1 teaspoon paprika  
- 1 teaspoon fine sea salt  
- 1 teaspoon onion powder  
- 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper  
- 2 full-size boneless skinless chicken breasts  
- 1/4 cup butter  
- 1 medium yellow onion, diced (about 1 cup)  
- 3 medium cloves garlic, minced  
- 1 1/2 teaspoons dried thyme leaves  
- 1 1/2 cups chicken broth (or more if preferred)  
- 1 1/2 pounds baby potatoes, halved  
- Handful of green beans  
- 1/4 cup all-purpose flour  
- 1 cup whole milk or half-and-half cream  

---

## Instructions

### 1. Season the Chicken

1. In a bowl, combine:
   - Olive oil  
   - Paprika  
   - Salt  
   - Onion powder  
   - Black pepper  

2. Slice the chicken breasts horizontally so you have 4 thinner pieces.  
3. Add chicken to the bowl and toss until evenly coated.

---

### 2. Sear the Chicken

1. Heat a Dutch oven over medium heat for about 4 minutes.  
2. Add the seasoned chicken and cook for about 2 minutes per side.  
3. Transfer chicken to a plate and set aside.

---

### 3. Build the Base

1. Add butter to the Dutch oven.  
2. Once melted, add diced onion and sauté for about 4 minutes.  
3. Stir in garlic and thyme; cook until fragrant.

---

### 4. Make the Sauce

1. Sprinkle flour over the onion mixture and stir thoroughly.  
2. Cook for 2 minutes.  
3. Slowly whisk in:
   - Chicken broth  
   - Milk or half-and-half  

4. Whisk until smooth and thickened.

---

### 5. Add Vegetables

1. Add baby potatoes and green beans.  
2. Increase heat and bring to a boil.  
3. Boil for about 4 minutes.  
4. Remove from heat.

---

### 6. Bake

1. Add the chicken back to the pot.  
2. Cover with the lid.  
3. Bake in a preheated 400°F oven for 15–17 minutes, or until:
   - Potatoes are fork-tender  
   - Chicken is fully cooked  

---

## Serving

Serve warm over egg noodles.

---

## Notes

- Add extra broth if you prefer a thinner sauce.  
- Fresh thyme may be substituted for dried (use ~1 tablespoon).  
- Ensure chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165°F (74°C).

---

**Prep Time:** ~10 minutes  
**Cook Time:** ~25–30 minutes  
**Total Time:** ~40 minutes  
**Servings:** 4
