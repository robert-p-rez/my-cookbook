# Creamy Italian Sausage and Gnocchi Soup

Rich, creamy, and hearty soup packed with Italian sausage, vegetables, gnocchi, spinach, and Parmesan.

## Ingredients

- 1 lb Italian sausage  
- 1 onion, diced  
- 2 carrots, diced  
- 2 ribs celery, diced  
- 6–7 garlic cloves, minced  
- 1/4 cup sun-dried tomatoes  
- 1 tsp herbs de Provence (or Italian seasoning)  
- 2 cups chicken broth  
- 1 lb gnocchi  
- 2 cups fresh chopped baby spinach  
- 2 cups heavy cream  
- 3 oz freshly grated Parmesan cheese  
- Salt and pepper, to taste  

---

## Instructions

1. In a large pot, brown the Italian sausage with diced onion, carrots, and celery until the meat is fully cooked and vegetables are softened.

2. Add minced garlic and sun-dried tomatoes. Cook for about 1 minute until fragrant.

3. Stir in herbs de Provence (or Italian seasoning), chicken broth, and gnocchi. Bring to a boil and cook for **2–3 minutes**.

4. Add fresh spinach and allow it to wilt.

5. Reduce heat to low. Stir in heavy cream and grated Parmesan.

6. Simmer on low for about **30 minutes**, until the soup thickens.

7. Season to taste with salt and pepper if needed. Serve warm.
