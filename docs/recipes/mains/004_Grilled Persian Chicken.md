# Grilled Persian Chicken

## Ingredients

- 1 1/2 oz. plain whole-milk yogurt  
- 2 tsp. kosher salt, plus more  
- 1 1/2 tsp. ground turmeric  
- 1 tsp. finely grated lime zest or ground sumac  
- 1 tsp. ground cinnamon  
- 1 tsp. ground cumin  
- 1/4 tsp. freshly ground black pepper, plus more  
- 1/4 tsp. ground cloves  
- 1/4 tsp. ground coriander  
- 1/8 tsp. cayenne pepper  
- 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs (about 1 1/2 lb.)  
- 4 whole chicken drumsticks (about 1 1/2 lb.)  
- 2 bone-in, skin-on chicken breasts (about 2 lb.), halved crosswise  
- 1 pint grape tomatoes  
- 1 lemon, quartered  
- Neutral oil, for brushing and grill  
- Steamed jasmine rice (optional)  
- Fresh cilantro, parsley, and mint leaves, for serving  

---

## Instructions

1. **Prepare the marinade:**  
   In a large bowl, mix yogurt, salt, turmeric, lime zest (or sumac), cinnamon, cumin, black pepper, cloves, coriander, and cayenne.

2. **Marinate the chicken:**  
   Add chicken pieces and turn to coat thoroughly. Transfer chicken and marinade to a resealable plastic bag.  
   Seal and refrigerate for at least **4 hours** or up to **24 hours**.

3. **Bring to room temperature:**  
   Let chicken sit at room temperature for **30 minutes** before grilling.  
   Thread tomatoes and lemon wedges onto skewers. Brush with oil and season with salt and pepper.

4. **Prepare the grill:**  
   Preheat grill to medium-high heat (about 5 minutes). Clean and oil grates.  
   Brush excess marinade off chicken; discard marinade.

5. **Grill the chicken:**  
   Place chicken skin side down and grill until skin is golden brown, about **15 minutes**.  
   Turn and continue grilling until skin is charred and chicken is cooked through.  
   Internal temperature should reach **160°F**, about **10 minutes more**.

6. **Rest:**  
   Transfer chicken to a plate and tent loosely with foil. Let rest for **10 minutes**.

7. **Grill skewers:**  
   Grill tomato and lemon skewers, turning occasionally, until charred in spots, **5–6 minutes**. Remove from skewers.

8. **Serve:**  
   Serve chicken and tomatoes with rice (if using).  
   Squeeze grilled lemon wedges over the top.  
   Garnish with cilantro, parsley, and mint.
