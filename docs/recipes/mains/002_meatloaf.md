# Classic Meatloaf

## Ingredients

- 2 pounds ground chuck  
- 1/2 cup crushed saltine crackers  
  *or Panko breadcrumbs*  
- 1 large egg  
- 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce  
- 2 tablespoons milk  
- 1 teaspoon salt  
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder  
- 1 teaspoon onion powder  
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper  

---

## Instructions

1. **Preheat the oven** to **350°F (175°C)**. Lightly grease a loaf pan or line a baking sheet with parchment paper.

2. **Prepare the binder:**  
   In a large bowl, combine the crushed saltine crackers and milk. Let them sit for 2–3 minutes to soften.

3. **Mix the meatloaf:**  
   Add the ground chuck, egg, Worcestershire sauce, salt, garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper to the bowl.  
   Using clean hands or a spoon, gently mix until the ingredients are just combined.  
   *Do not overmix, or the meatloaf may become dense.*

4. **Shape the loaf:**  
   Transfer the mixture to the prepared pan or baking sheet. Shape it into a tight, even loaf about 9 inches long.

5. **(Optional but recommended) Add a topping:**  
   Spread 1/2 cup ketchup or a mix of ketchup + BBQ sauce over the top.  
   For a more savory version, leave plain.

6. **Bake:**  
   Place in the oven and bake for **55–65 minutes**, or until the internal temperature reaches **160°F**.

7. **Rest the meatloaf:**  
   Remove from the oven and let it rest for **10 minutes** before slicing.  
   This helps the juices redistribute and keeps the slices from falling apart.
