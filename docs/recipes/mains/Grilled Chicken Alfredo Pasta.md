# Grilled Chicken Alfredo Pasta

**Source:** Secrets of Liya  
**Date:** 2023-06-02  

---

## Ingredients

### For the Chicken Marinade
- 3 boneless, skinless chicken breasts  
- 1 tbsp smoked paprika  
- 1/2 tbsp black pepper  
- 1/2 tbsp salt  
- 1/2 tbsp garlic powder  
- 1/2 tbsp dried parsley  
- A pinch of rosemary  

### Pasta
- Penne pasta (boiled according to package instructions)

### Alfredo Sauce
- 4 tbsp butter  
- 1/4 cup all-purpose flour  
- 8 oz milk  
- 1/2 cup heavy cream  
- 3 tbsp cream cheese  
- 1/3 cup mozzarella cheese  
- 1/3 cup parmesan cheese  
- 1 tbsp red chili flakes  
- Salt and pepper to taste  
- 1 tbsp dried parsley  
- 1 tbsp oregano  

---

## Instructions

### 1. Prepare and Grill the Chicken
1. Season the chicken breasts with smoked paprika, black pepper, salt, garlic powder, dried parsley, and rosemary.  
2. Grill for 6–8 minutes per side, or until fully cooked.  
3. Remove from heat and let rest for a few minutes.  
4. Slice into thin strips.

### 2. Make the Alfredo Sauce
1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.  
2. Add the flour and whisk until smooth, keeping the heat low.  
3. Slowly add the milk while stirring continuously.  
4. Add the cream cheese and heavy cream. Stir until smooth and creamy.  
5. Add mozzarella and parmesan cheese.  
6. Stir in red chili flakes, parsley, oregano, salt, and pepper to taste.  
7. Continue stirring until the sauce thickens and becomes smooth.

### 3. Assemble
1. Add the cooked penne pasta to the skillet and toss until fully coated in the Alfredo sauce.  
2. Top with sliced grilled chicken.  
3. Garnish with additional parsley if desired.

---

## Serve
Serve warm and enjoy.
