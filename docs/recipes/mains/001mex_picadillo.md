# 
Mexican Picadillo

This dinner is always on rotation at our house because it is so flavorful but comes together really easily. It is definitely a household favorite.

## Ingredients

- 1 green bell pepper
- 1 onion
- 3–4 small or 2 large potatoes
- 1 lb ground beef
- Chili powder, to taste
- Cumin, to taste
- Coriander, to taste
- Paprika, to taste
- Oregano, to taste
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Garlic, minced (to taste)
- 1 (15 oz) can beef broth
- 1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
- 1 bay leaf
- Green olives, sliced (optional)
- Cooked white rice, for serving
- Flour tortillas, for serving
- Fresh cilantro, for serving
{: .ingredients-cols}

## Instructions

1. Prep all vegetables (dice bell pepper, onion, and potatoes; mince garlic).
2. In a large pan, brown the ground beef over medium heat.  
   Drain excess grease if necessary.
3. Add the bell pepper, onion, potatoes, garlic, and all seasonings (chili powder, cumin, coriander, paprika, oregano, salt, and pepper).  
   Cook, stirring occasionally, until the vegetables start to soften.
4. Stir in the beef broth and tomato sauce.
5. Bring the mixture to a simmer and add the bay leaf.
6. Cover and cook for 10 minutes.
7. Remove the lid and simmer for an additional 10 minutes, or until the potatoes are tender and the mixture has slightly thickened.
8. Stir in green olives, if using.
9. Serve the picadillo over white rice with a warm flour tortilla and fresh cilantro.
