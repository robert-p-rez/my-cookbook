# Ground Beef and Cabbage Gratin

**Prep Time:** 10 minutes  
**Cook Time:** 50 minutes  
**Yield:** 4 servings  

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ground beef  
- 1 medium white cabbage, chopped  
- 1 yellow onion, chopped  
- 2 tablespoons olive oil  
- 1 teaspoon salt  
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper  
- 1/2 teaspoon paprika  
- Pinch of cayenne  

### For the Topping

- 3 eggs  
- 1 cup heavy cream  
- 1 cup grated parmesan cheese  

---

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to **350°F (175°C)**.

2. In a large oven-safe skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.  
   Add the ground beef and cook for 8–10 minutes, breaking it apart as it browns.

3. Add the chopped onion and sauté for 3–4 minutes until soft and translucent.

4. Stir in the chopped cabbage, salt, black pepper, paprika, and cayenne.  
   Cook for 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the cabbage softens and begins to brown slightly.

5. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, heavy cream, and grated parmesan cheese.

6. Pour the egg and cream mixture evenly over the beef and cabbage mixture in the skillet, ensuring the surface is covered.

7. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for **30–35 minutes**, or until the top is golden and bubbly and the center is set.

8. Let cool for 5–10 minutes before serving directly from the skillet.
