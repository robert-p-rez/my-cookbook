# Roasted Beet Borscht

A traditional beet soup with roasted beets for deeper flavor.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup celery, diced  
- 1 cup onions, diced  
- 1 cup cabbage, chopped  
- 2 cloves garlic, grated or pressed  
- 1 tablespoon butter (for sautéing vegetables)  
- 8 cups water or beef/chicken broth  
- 1 can diced tomatoes (28 oz or 1 quart home-canned)  
- 2 large beets or 3 medium beets  
- 1 large carrot or 2 medium, grated  
- 1 medium potato, diced  
- 1/2 cup fresh dill weed  
- Salt and black pepper, to taste  
- 1 bay leaf (optional)  
- Oil (for roasting beets)

---

## Instructions

### Roast the Beets

1. Preheat oven to **375°F**.
2. Lightly coat beets with oil.
3. Wrap beets in aluminum foil and place on a baking sheet.
4. Roast for **45–60 minutes**, until tender.
5. Let cool for 10 minutes, then peel.
6. Slice into 1/4-inch slices or dice as desired.

---

### Make the Soup

1. In a large pot, sauté onions, celery, and cabbage in butter until soft and translucent.
2. Add garlic and cook briefly.
3. Add diced tomatoes and broth. Bring to a boil.
4. Add roasted beets, grated carrots, diced potato, and bay leaf.
5. Reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are tender, about 15–20 minutes.
6. Stir in fresh dill, salt, and pepper.
7. Remove bay leaf before serving.

---

## Storage

- Cool completely before storing.
- Freeze up to 3 months in airtight containers.
- Thaw overnight and reheat gently on stovetop.
