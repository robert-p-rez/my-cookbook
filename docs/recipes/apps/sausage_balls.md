# Cream Cheese Sausage Balls

A rich, tender, cheesy sausage ball recipe using cream cheese for extra moisture and flavor.

## Ingredients

### For the Sausage Balls
- 1 lb spicy pork sausage, ground and at room temperature  
- 1 (8 oz) block cream cheese, softened  
- 2 cups sharp cheddar cheese, shredded  
  *or pepper jack for heat*  
- 1 1/4 cup Bisquick  
- 2 stalks scallions, finely chopped *(optional)*  
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder *(optional)*  

### For the Dipping Sauce
- 2 tablespoons mayo  
- 2 tablespoons sweet chili sauce  
- **OR** use honey mustard (as referenced in the original recipe)

---

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to **350°F (175°C)**. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.

2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the softened cream cheese and sausage.  
   Use your hands or a mixer to blend until evenly mixed.

3. Add shredded cheddar, Bisquick, scallions (if using), and garlic powder.  
   Mix until everything is fully incorporated and a uniform dough forms.

4. Roll the mixture into 1–1.5 inch balls and place them on the prepared baking sheet.

5. Bake for **20–25 minutes**, or until the sausage balls are golden brown and cooked through.

6. While baking, prepare the dipping sauce by mixing mayo and sweet chili sauce until smooth.

7. Serve warm with dipping sauce on the side.
