# Cheesy Thumbprint Appetizers with Hot Pepper Jelly

Savory cheddar thumbprint bites filled with sweet and spicy hot pepper jelly.

## Ingredients

- 1 cup all-purpose flour  
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened  
- 1 cup shredded sharp cheddar cheese  
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder  
- 1/4 teaspoon salt  
- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper  
- 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard (optional)  
- 1/4 cup hot pepper jelly (or more to taste)  

---

## Instructions

1. **Preheat oven** to **350°F (175°C)**. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper or lightly grease it.

2. **Make the dough:**  
   In a large bowl, combine softened butter, flour, shredded cheddar cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Add Dijon mustard if using. Mix until a smooth, cohesive dough forms.

3. **Shape:**  
   Roll dough into 1-inch balls and place on prepared baking sheet about 1 inch apart.

4. **Create indentations:**  
   Use your thumb or the back of a spoon to press a small well into the center of each ball.

5. **Bake:**  
   Bake for **12–15 minutes**, until edges are golden brown.

6. **Add jelly:**  
   Let cool for about 5 minutes. Spoon approximately 1/2 teaspoon hot pepper jelly into each indentation.

7. **Serve:**  
   Allow jelly to set slightly. Serve warm or at room temperature.

---

**Prep Time:** 10 minutes  
**Cook Time:** 15 minutes  
**Total Time:** 25 minutes  
**Servings:** 24 appetizers
